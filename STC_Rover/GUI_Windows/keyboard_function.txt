up arrow:       forward
down arrow:     backwards
d:              spin on dime
s:              change spin direction
g:              change gear speed

Pressing up/down/d overwrites current direction, even if another key is pressed. 
    example: hold forward, goes forward. Press backwards while holding forward, goes backwards. 

Gear changes speed from 1, 2, 3. Wrap around. Controls motor speed. 
S will change the direction of the spinning motion.
